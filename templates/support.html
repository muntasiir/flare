Welcome to the support page of our telecom app. Here you can find resources and assistance to help you with any issues or inquiries you may have.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Will I get charged data overage fees for going above limit?

Yes, you will be charged $20 for every GB over the limit.

I am not getting the desired internet speed?

Try these few troubleshooting tips:

  1. Remove any obstruction near the modem.
  2. Check if the modem is still in-date. Place an order for a new one.
  3. Check the cables.

How can I change my subscription plan?

You can change your subscription plan by visiting the 'Account Settings' section in your profile.

Do you offer discounts for long-term subscriptions?

Yes, we offer discounts for annual subscription plans. Contact our customer support for more details.

What should I do if my device is lost or stolen?

If your device is lost or stolen, please contact us immediately to suspend your service and prevent unauthorized usage.

Contact Us
